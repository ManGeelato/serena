About Serenapark Old Age Nursing Home

We care for your elderly!

01

Who are we?

Serenapark Old Age Nursing Home was founded in 1999, with the goal of taking care of the frail, disabled and wounded. We pride ourselves in ensuring every individual at our home feels safe, loved and well taken of. We boast of 3 branches all over South Africa and offer nothing but the best of service it is to offer.

02

What do you get at Serenapark?

At Serenapark Old Age Nursing Home, we offer all services not limited to Assisted Living, Independence Living, Frail Care, Disabled Living, Recreational Activites and many more. Our homes are designed to include each and every person with any background disability. Our vibrant community offers a range of engaging activities and top-notch amenities designed to enhance your quality of life.

03

Our team

Our home boasts of only the best caregivers your loved ones can get. Each caregiver we employ has got a special place for the elderly as this is a priority at our home. Over 60 skilled professionals are committed to delivering exceptional care and service to our residents. Each member of our team brings a wealth of expertise and a genuine passion for enhancing the lives of our seniors.
